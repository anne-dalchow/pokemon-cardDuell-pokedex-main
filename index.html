Game Instruction

In this Pokémon Duel Card Game, you'll face off against the computer in a battle of strategy and skill over 7 intense rounds.
Both you and the computer are dealt 6 random Pokémon cards, each with different stats like HP, Speed, and more.
The game randomly selects which stat to compare in each round, such as HP or Speed,
and the computer lays down its card first, revealing its stat.

Your challenge is to choose a card from your hand that has a higher value in the required stat to win the round.
But be careful! You won't always have the higher numbers, and sometimes you'll need to think ahead and play tactically.
Knowing when to use your strongest cards and when to hold back for later rounds can make all the difference.

The goal is simple: win as many duels as possible by outsmarting the computer.
With each round, you'll feel the tension rise as you get closer to the final result.
Will you emerge victorious with the most duel wins, or will the computer outplay you?


It's time to shuffle the deck and find out!
